Javier Milei, new President of Argentina

This Sunday, November 19, Javier Milei (La Libertad Avanza) was elected president of Argentina. He won the runoff or second round with 55.69% against Sergio Massa (Unión por la Patria), who obtained 44.30%, according to the results of the provisional scrutiny. The second round was the closing of an electoral process that began in the PASO on August 13 with 27 candidates from 15 political fronts and continued with the general elections on October 22, in which no candidate obtained 45% or 40%. and 10 percentage points of votes over the next person with the most support, the requirements demanded by the National Constitution to be consecrated president-elect in that instance without having to go to the second round.

Weakening Growth, Financial Risks

Global growth is projected to slow significantly amid high inflation, tight monetary policy, and more restrictive credit conditions. The possibility of more widespread bank turmoil and tighter monetary policy could result in even weaker global growth and lead to financial dislocations in the most vulnerable emerging market and developing economies (EMDEs). Comprehensive policy action is needed to foster macroeconomic and financial stability. Among many EMDEs, and especially in low-income countries, bolstering fiscal sustainability will require generating higher revenues, making spending more efficient, and improving debt management practices. Continued international cooperation is also necessary to tackle climate change, support populations affected by crises and hunger, and provide debt relief where needed.

Mercado Libre accelerates sales

The company's net sales amounted to USD 2.7 billion, a growth of 61% in constant currency. The volume of merchandise sold by Mercado Libre reaches USD 8.6 billion, a year-on-year increase of 32%. The total volume of payments processed by Mercado Pago was USD 32.2 billion, an increase of 76%. The operating result was USD 296 million, with a margin of 11%. With 4 million new active users, the base reached 88 million at the end of the quarter.
